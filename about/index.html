My Story

My journey into digital marketing began 5 years ago when I discovered the incredible potential of Facebook advertising for small businesses. What started as curiosity quickly became a passion as I saw how the right marketing strategy could transform a struggling business into a thriving success story.

Over the years, I've specialized in Meta advertising (Facebook and Instagram ads) and have become an early adopter of AI marketing tools. I believe that combining human creativity with artificial intelligence creates the most powerful marketing campaigns.

Today, I help businesses of all sizes achieve their growth goals through data-driven marketing strategies, creative campaign development, and continuous optimization. My approach is always personalized because I understand that every business is unique.
