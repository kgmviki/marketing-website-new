Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

How quickly can we get started?

After our initial consultation and agreement on strategy, I can typically begin work within 1-2 weeks. For urgent projects, I may be able to start sooner.

Do you work with businesses in my industry?

I work with businesses across various industries including e-commerce, SaaS, professional services, healthcare, and more. My strategies are adaptable to different business models.

What's included in your reporting?

You'll receive detailed monthly reports covering campaign performance, ROI analysis, key metrics, insights, and recommendations for optimization.

Do you require long-term contracts?

I typically work on month-to-month agreements after an initial 3-month commitment. This ensures we have enough time to see results while maintaining flexibility.

How do you measure success?

Success is measured based on your specific goals - whether that's increased revenue, lead generation, brand awareness, or other KPIs we define together.

What makes you different from agencies?

As a freelancer, you get direct access to me, personalized attention, faster communication, and often better value. I'm personally invested in your success.
